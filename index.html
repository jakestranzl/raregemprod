About

Jake Stranzl is a filmmaker and editor running Rare Gem Productions out of Idaho. He makes films for fishing lodges, outfitters, and outdoor brands across the West — from hero videos that sell a season to short docs that tell the story behind the place.

Before going independent he worked at GoPro and the Boston Red Sox, bringing that same level of craft to every project since.
